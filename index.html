HOCUS POCUS NUMBER GUESSING GAME!!

How to play

  1. Guess any number between 1-20 (1 & 20 included)
  2. Enter number in 'Guess a number field' and click Submit Guess button!
  3. You'll fave a total of 4 guesses!
  4. If you do not get the number after 4 guesses, click on Play Again button and try again!
